Sacha FENESTRAZ (#23, FEN) - Nissan Formula E Team
Sacha Fenestraz, representing France, is a Nissan Formula E Team driver known for his speed, with one career pole position.

Sacha Fenestraz, a French driver, competes for the Nissan Formula E Team in car number 23. He began his Formula E career in the Seoul season and has since made 31 career starts. Throughout his time in the series, Fenestraz has accumulated 58 career points.

While he has yet to secure a win or a podium finish, Fenestraz has demonstrated his pace by achieving one career pole position. He consistently aims to convert his qualifying speed into strong race performances.

For Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix, Fenestraz is driving the Nissan e-4ORCE 04 for the Nissan Formula E Team, starting from grid position 17. His teammate at Nissan is Oliver Rowland, who drives car number 22.

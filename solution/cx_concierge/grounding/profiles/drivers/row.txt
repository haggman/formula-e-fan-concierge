Oliver ROWLAND (#22, ROW) - Nissan Formula E Team
Oliver Rowland, a British driver for Nissan, is known for his qualifying prowess and consistent points scoring in Formula E.

Oliver Rowland, representing Great Britain, competes for the Nissan Formula E Team in car number 22. He is a seasoned competitor in the series, having made 76 career starts prior to this event. Throughout his Formula E career, which began in the Punta del Este season, Rowland has secured 2 wins, 12 podium finishes, and 8 pole positions, accumulating a total of 403 points.

Rowland is recognized for his speed over a single lap, often placing his car at the sharp end of the grid. His ability to extract maximum performance during qualifying sessions has been a hallmark of his time in Formula E, contributing significantly to his career achievements.

For Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix, Rowland will be starting from grid position 16 in his Nissan e-4ORCE 04. He continues his tenure with the Nissan Formula E Team, driving for the Japanese manufacturer. His teammate in the sister car, number 23, is Sacha Fenestraz.

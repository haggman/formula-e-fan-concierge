Nick CASSIDY (#37, CAS) - Jaguar TCS Racing
Nick Cassidy, a formidable New Zealander, brings a wealth of experience and a strong record to Jaguar TCS Racing.

Nick Cassidy, hailing from New Zealand, is a key driver for Jaguar TCS Racing, piloting the number 37 Jaguar I-TYPE 6. He has established himself as a significant competitor in Formula E since his debut season in Diriyah, accumulating 61 career starts.

Throughout his Formula E career, Cassidy has demonstrated consistent performance, securing 7 wins and achieving 20 podium finishes. His speed is also evident in his 5 career pole positions and 5 fastest laps, contributing to a total of 510 career points.

For Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix, Cassidy is set to start from grid position 2. He continues to race alongside his teammate, Mitch Evans, who drives car number 9, forming a strong pairing for the Jaguar manufacturer.

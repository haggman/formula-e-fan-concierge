Jake HUGHES (#5, HUG) - NEOM McLaren Formula E Team
Jake Hughes, representing NEOM McLaren, brings a strong qualifying record and a podium finish to his Formula E career.

Jake Hughes, hailing from Great Britain, competes for the NEOM McLaren Formula E Team in car number 5. Known for his impressive qualifying pace, Hughes has made 29 career starts in Formula E, accumulating 94 points. His debut season in the series was in Mexico City.

Throughout his Formula E journey, Hughes has secured 4 pole positions, showcasing his ability to extract maximum performance over a single lap. He has also achieved 1 podium finish, demonstrating his potential to contend at the front of the pack.

For Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix, Jake Hughes is driving the Nissan e-4ORCE 04, powered by Nissan, and will start from grid position 14. His teammate at NEOM McLaren is Taylor Barnard, who drives car number 8.

Paul ARON (#16, ARO) - Envision Racing
Paul Aron is an Estonian driver making his mark in Formula E with Envision Racing, driving the No. 16 Jaguar I-TYPE 6.

Paul Aron, representing Estonia, joins Envision Racing for the Berlin E-Prix, driving the No. 16 Jaguar I-TYPE 6. He is a new face in Formula E, making his debut in the current Berlin season. This event marks his third career start in the championship.

Prior to this race, Aron has made two career starts in Formula E. He is still seeking his first career win, podium, pole position, and fastest lap in the series, having yet to score points in his nascent Formula E career.

For this Round 10 event in Berlin, Paul Aron starts from grid position 19. He is partnered at Envision Racing by teammate Joel Eriksson, who drives the No. 4 car, both competing with the Jaguar I-TYPE 6.

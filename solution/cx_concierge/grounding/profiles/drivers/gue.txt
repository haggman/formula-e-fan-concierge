Maximilian GÜNTHER (#7, GUE) - Maserati MSG Racing
Maximilian Günther, representing Maserati MSG Racing, is a proven race winner and a formidable competitor in Formula E.

Maximilian Günther, a German driver, competes with car number 7 for Maserati MSG Racing. He has established himself as a significant talent in the all-electric series since his debut in the Diriyah season. Günther is known for his aggressive driving style and ability to convert strong qualifying performances into race results.

Throughout his Formula E career, Günther has made 81 starts, accumulating 5 wins and 10 podium finishes. He has also secured 2 pole positions, demonstrating his one-lap pace. These performances have contributed to a career total of 335 points, marking him as a consistent points scorer in the championship.

For the Berlin 2024 E-Prix, Round 10, Günther is driving the Maserati Tipo Folgore, powered by the STELLANTIS manufacturer, and will start from grid position 5. His teammate at Maserati MSG Racing is Jehan Daruvala, who drives car number 18. Günther continues to be a key figure for the team, aiming to add to his impressive career statistics.

Jean-Éric VERGNE (#25, JEV) - DS PENSKE
Jean-Éric Vergne, a French racing veteran, is a two-time Formula E champion known for his strategic prowess.

Jean-Éric Vergne, representing France, competes for DS PENSKE in car number 25. He is one of the most experienced drivers in Formula E history, having made 128 career starts since his debut in Punta del Este. Throughout his career, Vergne has consistently been a front-runner, accumulating 11 wins, 36 podium finishes, and 17 pole positions.

His impressive track record also includes 6 fastest laps and a substantial 1123 career points, underscoring his consistent performance and competitive edge in the all-electric series. Vergne's strategic approach and ability to perform under pressure have cemented his status as a formidable competitor in the championship.

For this event, Vergne is driving the DS E-TENSE FE23, powered by the STELLANTIS manufacturer. He starts from grid position 9, aiming to leverage his extensive experience. His teammate at DS PENSKE is Stoffel Vandoorne, who drives car number 2.

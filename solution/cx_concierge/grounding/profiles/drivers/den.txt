Jake DENNIS (#1, DEN) - Andretti Formula E
Jake Dennis, the British racing talent, brings his championship-winning experience to Andretti Formula E.

Jake Dennis, hailing from Great Britain, competes for Andretti Formula E in car number 1. He is a seasoned competitor in Formula E, having made 61 career starts since his debut in the Diriyah season. Throughout his career, Dennis has accumulated an impressive record, including 6 wins, 21 podium finishes, and 6 pole positions. He has also secured 10 fastest laps and a total of 568 career points, showcasing his consistent performance and speed on track.

For the Berlin 2024 E-Prix, Dennis is driving the Porsche 99X Electric for Andretti Formula E, starting from grid position 1. His partnership with the Porsche manufacturer underscores the team's commitment to high-performance racing in the series.

Dennis is known for his aggressive yet precise driving style, often demonstrating strong race craft and strategic awareness. His ability to extract maximum performance from his machinery has made him a formidable opponent on various street circuits around the world.

At Andretti Formula E, Jake Dennis is teamed with Norman Nato, who drives car number 17. Together, they form a competitive duo aiming to secure strong results for the Porsche-powered team.

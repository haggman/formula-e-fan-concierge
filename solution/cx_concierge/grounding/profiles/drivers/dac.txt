António Félix DA COSTA (#13, DAC) - TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team
António Félix da Costa, a seasoned Portuguese racer, brings a wealth of experience and a competitive spirit to the TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team.

António Félix da Costa, hailing from Portugal, is a prominent figure in Formula E, currently driving the number 13 Porsche 99X Electric for the TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team. Known for his aggressive yet precise driving style, da Costa has been a consistent front-runner throughout his extensive career in the all-electric series.

His Formula E journey began in the inaugural Putrajaya season, and since then, he has amassed an impressive record. Over 126 career starts, da Costa has secured 12 wins and stood on the podium 23 times. He has also demonstrated his qualifying prowess with 8 pole positions and recorded 4 fastest laps, accumulating a total of 801 career points.

For Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix, António Félix da Costa is set to start from grid position 10. He continues to compete alongside his teammate Pascal Wehrlein, who drives car number 94, as part of the TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team, utilizing the Porsche 99X Electric.

Da Costa's experience and proven track record make him a formidable competitor, always a threat to challenge at the sharp end of the grid. His long-standing presence in Formula E underscores his dedication and skill in the demanding world of electric motorsport.

Mitch EVANS (#9, EVA) - Jaguar TCS Racing
Mitch Evans, a seasoned New Zealander for Jaguar TCS Racing, is a consistent front-runner in Formula E.

Mitch Evans, representing New Zealand, competes for Jaguar TCS Racing in the #9 Jaguar I-TYPE 6. A veteran of the series, he has made 109 career starts in Formula E, dating back to the Hong Kong season. Over his career, Evans has accumulated an impressive 12 wins, 29 podium finishes, and 8 pole positions, amassing a total of 888 points.

Known for his aggressive yet precise driving style, Evans has established himself as a formidable competitor capable of challenging for victories on any given race weekend. His tenure with Jaguar has seen him become one of the most recognizable and successful drivers for the British manufacturer.

For Round 10 of the Berlin 2024 E-Prix, Mitch Evans starts from grid position 4. He continues to drive for Jaguar TCS Racing, piloting their Jaguar-manufactured vehicle. His teammate at Jaguar TCS Racing is Nick Cassidy, who drives car #37.

Stoffel VANDOORNE (#2, VAN) - DS PENSKE
Stoffel Vandoorne, a Belgian former champion, brings a wealth of experience and skill to DS PENSKE.

Stoffel Vandoorne, hailing from Belgium, is a prominent figure in Formula E, currently competing for DS PENSKE in the #2 DS E-TENSE FE23. He began his Formula E journey in the Diriyah season and has since established a strong record in the championship. Over 85 career starts, Vandoorne has secured 3 wins, achieved 16 podium finishes, and claimed 8 pole positions, accumulating a total of 528 points.

Known for his precise driving style and strategic race management, Vandoorne has consistently been a contender at the sharp end of the grid. His experience across multiple seasons has made him a formidable competitor, capable of extracting maximum performance from his machinery.

For this event, Stoffel Vandoorne is driving for the DS PENSKE team, utilizing the STELLANTIS-manufactured DS E-TENSE FE23. He will be starting from grid position 8. His teammate at DS PENSKE is Jean-Éric Vergne, who drives car #25, forming a potent pairing for the team.

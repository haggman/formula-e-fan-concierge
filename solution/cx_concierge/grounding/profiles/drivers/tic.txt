Dan TICKTUM (#33, TIC) - ERT Formula E Team
Dan Ticktum, representing Great Britain, brings his aggressive racing style to the ERT Formula E Team.

Dan Ticktum, hailing from Great Britain, competes for the ERT Formula E Team in the #33 ERT X24. Known for his assertive driving, Ticktum has been a presence in Formula E since his debut in the Diriyah season.

Throughout his Formula E career, Ticktum has made 46 starts, accumulating 41 career points. While he has yet to secure a win, podium, pole position, or fastest lap, his consistent participation demonstrates his dedication to the sport.

For Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix, Ticktum starts from 22nd position on the grid. He continues to drive for the Electric Racing Technologies manufacturer. His teammate at ERT is Sérgio Sette Câmara, who pilots car #3.

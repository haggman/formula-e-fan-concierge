Edoardo MORTARA (#48, MOR) - MAHINDRA RACING
Edoardo Mortara, a seasoned Swiss competitor, brings a wealth of experience and multiple victories to Mahindra Racing.

Edoardo Mortara, representing Switzerland, is a prominent figure in Formula E, currently driving car number 48 for Mahindra Racing. He embarked on his Formula E journey in the Hong Kong season, quickly establishing himself as a formidable competitor. Throughout his extensive career, Mortara has participated in 93 starts, securing 6 wins, 13 podium finishes, and 3 pole positions, accumulating a total of 441 career points. He also boasts 2 fastest laps, underscoring his consistent pace and racecraft.

For Round 10 of the Berlin 2024 E-Prix, Mortara is piloting the Mahindra M10Electro, manufactured by Mahindra Racing. He will be starting the race from grid position 7, aiming to leverage his considerable experience from a strong starting spot.

Mortara is known for his precise driving style and strategic race management, often finding opportunities to advance through the field. His career statistics highlight his ability to not only contend for podiums but also convert strong performances into victories.

At Mahindra Racing, Edoardo Mortara's teammate for this event is Jordan King, who drives car number 21. Together, they form the driver lineup for the Mahindra Racing team, working to maximize the performance of the Mahindra M10Electro.

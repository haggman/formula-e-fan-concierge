Norman NATO (#17, NAT) - Andretti Formula E
Norman Nato, representing France, brings a wealth of experience and a proven track record to Andretti Formula E.

Norman Nato, the French driver, competes for Andretti Formula E in the #17 Porsche 99X Electric. He enters Round 10 of the Berlin E-Prix starting from third on the grid. Nato is a seasoned competitor in Formula E, having made 47 career starts since his debut in the Diriyah season.

Throughout his Formula E career, Nato has demonstrated his capability with 1 win and 3 podium finishes. He has also secured 4 fastest laps, showcasing his raw speed on track. With a total of 163 career points, Nato has consistently contributed to his teams' efforts over the seasons.

For this event, Nato is driving for Andretti Formula E, powered by the Porsche manufacturer. He is partnered with teammate Jake Dennis, who pilots car #1, forming a strong lineup for the team. Nato's experience and past successes position him as a key contender in the competitive field.

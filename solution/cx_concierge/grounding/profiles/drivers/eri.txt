Joel ERIKSSON (#4, ERI) - Envision Racing
Joel Eriksson, a Swedish driver for Envision Racing, enters the Berlin E-Prix aiming to build on his Formula E experience.

Joel Eriksson, representing Sweden, drives the number 4 Jaguar I-TYPE 6 for Envision Racing. He is set to start from 11th position on the grid for this event. Eriksson's Formula E career began in the Puebla season, and he has since made 10 career starts, accumulating 3 points. He currently has no wins, podiums, pole positions, or fastest laps in the series.

Eriksson is known for his competitive spirit and has been working to establish himself in the demanding world of Formula E. His previous appearances have provided valuable experience, and he continues to push for stronger results with each race.

For the Berlin E-Prix, Eriksson is part of the Envision Racing team, which utilizes Jaguar powertrains. He will be competing alongside his teammate, Paul Aron, who drives car number 16. The event offers another opportunity for Eriksson to showcase his skills and contribute to his team's performance.

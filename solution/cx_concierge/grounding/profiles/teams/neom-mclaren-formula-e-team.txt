NEOM McLaren Formula E Team
NEOM McLaren Formula E Team, powered by Nissan, fields a promising driver lineup for Season 10.

The NEOM McLaren Formula E Team competes with powertrains supplied by Nissan, showcasing a blend of iconic racing heritage and advanced electric vehicle technology. For the 2024 season, the team's car numbers are 5 and 8.

Their driver lineup for Season 10 features Jake Hughes in car #5 and Taylor Barnard in car #8. This pairing brings a combined career total of 99 points to the team.

While the current lineup has secured one podium finish in their combined Formula E careers, they are still seeking their first victory, highlighting their ambition to climb the ranks in the competitive championship.

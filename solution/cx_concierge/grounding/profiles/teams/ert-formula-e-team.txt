ERT Formula E Team
ERT Formula E Team, powered by Electric Racing Technologies, fields a determined driver lineup for Season 10.

The ERT Formula E Team competes in the ABB FIA Formula E World Championship, utilizing powertrains developed by its in-house manufacturer, Electric Racing Technologies. This vertical integration allows the team to closely develop and refine its racing technology.

For the 2024 season, the ERT Formula E Team features a driver pairing of Sérgio Sette Câmara, driving car number 3, and Dan Ticktum, behind the wheel of car number 33. Both drivers are focused on making their mark in the competitive Formula E landscape.

Collectively, Sette Câmara and Ticktum bring a combined career total of 84 points to the team. While they are still pursuing their first career wins and podium finishes in Formula E, their presence underscores ERT's commitment to developing talent and pushing the boundaries of electric motorsport.

Jaguar TCS Racing
Jaguar TCS Racing, powered by Jaguar, fields a formidable driver lineup for the Berlin E-Prix.

Jaguar TCS Racing is the factory team for the iconic British manufacturer, Jaguar. They develop their own powertrain, showcasing Jaguar's commitment to electric vehicle technology and performance on the global stage of Formula E. The team is a consistent presence in the championship, aiming to demonstrate the capabilities of their electric racing platform.

For the 2024 season, Jaguar TCS Racing features a strong driver pairing with Mitch Evans in car #9 and Nick Cassidy in car #37. Both drivers are known for their competitive spirit and skill on track.

Together, Evans and Cassidy bring significant experience and success to the team. Their combined career statistics in Formula E include an impressive 19 wins, 49 podium finishes, and a total of 1398 points, highlighting their proven ability to contend at the front of the field.

Envision Racing
Envision Racing, powered by Jaguar, fields a dynamic new driver lineup for Season 10.

Envision Racing, known for its distinctive green livery, competes in Formula E with powertrains supplied by Jaguar. The team has established itself as a prominent contender in the championship, consistently showcasing competitive performance.

For the 2024 season, Envision Racing features a new driver pairing: Joel Eriksson, driving car number 4, and Paul Aron, behind the wheel of car number 16. This fresh lineup brings new energy to the team.

As of the Berlin 2024 E-Prix, this driver combination has accumulated a total of 3 career points in Formula E, with neither driver having yet secured a win or a podium finish in the series.

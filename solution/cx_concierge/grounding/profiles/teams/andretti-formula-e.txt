Andretti Formula E
Andretti Formula E, powered by Porsche, brings a formidable driver lineup to Berlin with a history of success.

Andretti Formula E competes with powertrains supplied by Porsche, combining the American racing giant's heritage with German engineering. The team fields two cars, with Jake Dennis piloting car #1 and Norman Nato in car #17 for the 2024 season.

This driver pairing represents a significant force on the track, bringing a wealth of experience to the team. Together, Dennis and Nato have accumulated an impressive seven career wins in Formula E. Their combined efforts have also resulted in a substantial 24 podium finishes, showcasing their consistent ability to challenge at the front.

With a total of 731 career points between them, the Andretti Formula E lineup demonstrates a strong track record of performance and a capacity to score valuable points in the highly competitive Formula E championship.

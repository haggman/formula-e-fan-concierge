TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team
The TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team brings a legacy of motorsport excellence to the all-electric championship.

The TAG Heuer Porsche Formula E Team competes with its proprietary Porsche powertrain, embodying the brand's commitment to innovation and performance in electric racing. As a manufacturer team, Porsche designs and builds its own electric motors and related components, aiming for optimal efficiency and speed on the track.

For the 2024 season, the team fields a strong driver line-up featuring António Félix da Costa, racing with car number 13, and Pascal Wehrlein, behind the wheel of car number 94. Both drivers are established talents in Formula E, bringing significant experience to the team.

Together, da Costa and Wehrlein represent a formidable pairing, boasting a combined career total of 18 wins and 33 podium finishes in Formula E. Their collective efforts have also accumulated 1327 career points, highlighting their consistent performance and competitive edge in the series.

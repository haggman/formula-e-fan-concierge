DS PENSKE
DS PENSKE combines French automotive excellence with a formidable driver lineup, aiming for top performance.

DS PENSKE represents the collaboration between the French luxury automotive brand DS Automobiles, part of the STELLANTIS group, and the American racing team PENSKE. This partnership brings together a rich heritage in motorsport with cutting-edge electric vehicle technology, as DS Automobiles provides the powertrain for the team's entries. The team competes with a strong focus on innovation and performance in the competitive world of Formula E.

For the 2024 season, DS PENSKE fields a highly experienced driver lineup. Car number 2 is piloted by Stoffel Vandoorne, while car number 25 is driven by Jean-Éric Vergne. Both drivers are well-known figures in Formula E, bringing a wealth of racing expertise to the team.

Together, Stoffel Vandoorne and Jean-Éric Vergne boast an impressive combined career record in Formula E. Their combined statistics include 14 career wins, 52 podium finishes, and a total of 1651 points accumulated throughout their time in the championship. This demonstrates a consistent track record of success and a deep understanding of the unique demands of electric street racing.

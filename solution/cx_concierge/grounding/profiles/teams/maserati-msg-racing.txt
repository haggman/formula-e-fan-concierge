Maserati MSG Racing
Maserati MSG Racing brings a storied automotive name to Formula E, powered by STELLANTIS.

Maserati MSG Racing represents a significant presence in Formula E, carrying the iconic Maserati nameplate. As part of the STELLANTIS group, the team benefits from a rich heritage in motorsport and automotive innovation, aiming to translate that legacy into electric racing success.

For the 2024 season, Maserati MSG Racing fields a driver lineup consisting of Maximilian Günther, piloting car number 7, and Jehan Daruvala, behind the wheel of car number 18. This pairing combines experience with emerging talent as they navigate the competitive Formula E landscape.

Together, Günther and Daruvala bring a combined career total of 5 wins and 10 podium finishes to the team. Their collective efforts have also accumulated 343 points over their Formula E careers, showcasing their ability to consistently contend for strong results.

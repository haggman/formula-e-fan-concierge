Nissan Formula E Team
The Nissan Formula E Team, powered by Nissan, fields a dynamic driver duo for Season 10, aiming for strong performances.

The Nissan Formula E Team competes in the ABB FIA Formula E World Championship, representing the Japanese manufacturer Nissan. Known for its global automotive presence, Nissan provides the powertrain for its factory team, bringing its engineering expertise to the all-electric racing series.

For the 2024 season, the Nissan Formula E Team features a driver line-up consisting of Oliver Rowland, driving car number 22, and Sacha Fenestraz, in car number 23. This pairing brings a blend of experience and emerging talent to the grid.

Together, Rowland and Fenestraz have accumulated a combined career total of 2 wins, 12 podium finishes, and 461 points in Formula E. These statistics highlight their capability to contend for strong results and contribute to the team's ambitions throughout the season.

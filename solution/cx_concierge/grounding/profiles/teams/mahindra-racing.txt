MAHINDRA RACING
Mahindra Racing, a founding Formula E team, competes with its own powertrain, driven by a seasoned 2024 lineup.

Mahindra Racing is a founding team in Formula E, having been part of the championship since its inaugural season. The team operates as both a constructor and a race team, developing its own powertrain for competition. For the 2024 season, Mahindra Racing fields two cars: car #21 and car #48.

The 2024 driver lineup features Jordan King in car #21 and Edoardo Mortara in car #48. This pairing brings a wealth of experience to the team. Together, King and Mortara have accumulated 6 career wins and 13 podium finishes in Formula E.

Their combined efforts have resulted in a total of 441 career points, showcasing a strong competitive background as they navigate the challenges of the Berlin E-Prix.

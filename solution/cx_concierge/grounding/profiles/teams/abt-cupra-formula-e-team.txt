ABT CUPRA FORMULA E TEAM
ABT CUPRA is a team with a long history in motorsport, powered by Mahindra Racing for the 2024 season.

The ABT CUPRA Formula E Team, known for its established presence in motorsport, competes in the 2024 Formula E season utilizing powertrains supplied by Mahindra Racing. This collaboration brings together ABT's racing expertise with Mahindra's engineering.

For the 2024 season, ABT CUPRA fields a driver line-up consisting of Lucas di Grassi, piloting car number 11, and Kelvin van der Linde, behind the wheel of car number 51. This pairing brings a wealth of experience to the team.

Together, Lucas di Grassi and Kelvin van der Linde have accumulated an impressive 13 career wins, 40 podium finishes, and a combined total of 1043 points in their Formula E careers, highlighting their competitive pedigree.

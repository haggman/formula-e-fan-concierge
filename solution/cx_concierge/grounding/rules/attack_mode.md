# Attack Mode

**Topic:** Attack Mode · **Scope:** Formula E Gen3, Berlin 2024 E-Prix (Season 10, Rounds 9 & 10)

Attack Mode is Formula E's signature strategic overtaking aid. A driver arms it, then collects it by steering **off the racing line through a marked activation zone** — at the Berlin Tempelhof circuit that zone is on the **outside of Turn 2**. Taking the longer route through the zone costs track position in the moment, which is the trade-off: a driver gives up time on the lap they activate in exchange for a temporary power boost they hope to convert into passes.

When active, Attack Mode raises the car's maximum power from the **300 kW race baseline to 350 kW — a +50 kW boost** (roughly +67 bhp). The boost makes a real, visible difference, especially on acceleration out of slow corners and on the run to a braking zone, and it usually shows up in the data as a noticeable bump in a car's top speed for the laps it is deployed.

Each driver must take a **mandated total amount** of Attack Mode time during the race, and failing to use the full allocation brings a penalty. The total budget is **set per event by the FIA** and was **reduced for Berlin**: Round 9 (Saturday) used a **6-minute** total and Round 10 (Sunday) used a **4-minute** total, down from the more typical 8 minutes, per the FIA's 11 May 2024 statement. Drivers split that budget across **two activations**.

For Round 10, the 4-minute (240-second) budget is split across two activations in one of three pre-selected **scenarios**: **scenario 1 = short-first (60 s + 180 s)**, **scenario 2 = even (120 s + 120 s)**, and **scenario 3 = long-first (180 s + 60 s)**. Drivers can change which scenario they intend to use by re-arming before they activate, so a sequence of scenario "arming" events is a window into a driver's strategic intent — repeatedly arming scenario 3, for example, signals they are considering going long on their first deployment. The actual deployment (activation) is distinct from arming: arming is intent, activation is the car drawing the extra power.

**Quick facts:** +50 kW boost (300 → 350 kW) · activation zone at the outside of Turn 2 · R10 budget 4 min total / two activations · R09 budget 6 min · scenarios 1/2/3 = 60+180, 120+120, 180+60 seconds.
